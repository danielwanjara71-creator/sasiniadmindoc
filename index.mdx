# Introduction

The **Sasini Admin Dashboard** is the web-based management centre for the Sasini PLC platform. It lets authorised staff manage farmer accounts, track produce pickups, oversee Agri-Shop orders, broadcast communications, monitor pest threats, and control all aspects of the farmer-facing mobile experience — in real time, from one place.

<Note>
It is **desktop-only** — minimum screen width 991px. Always use a laptop or desktop computer. Trying to access from a phone or small tablet shows a blocking warning.
</Note>
